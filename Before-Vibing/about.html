Our Living Heritage

The Transformed Evolution: Esther Heritage Specialist Hospital

IconCare Specialist Clinic Ltd was founded on the ultimate selflessness and comprehensive care ethos driven by our late founder. Today, her inspiring medical legacy transitions and blossoms gracefully through her three children—all thoroughly qualified, brilliant, certified clinical doctors—who have enthusiastically taken up the mantle to direct the hospital's clinical parameters following her demise.

"Under her eternal guiding ethos, we don't just treat illnesses; we honor a beautiful life's mission of restoring health."

In profound honor of this historical blueprint and familial commitment, the clinic operates its main evolution and subsidiary wings under the name: Esther Heritage Specialist Hospital. This combined healthcare enterprise offers clinical scope stretching across ultra-modern laboratory testing/diagnostics to comprehensive multi-departmental medical consultation panels.

The Medical Directors & Siblings carrying the Esther Heritage vision.
